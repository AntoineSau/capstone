How to play categories?

  1. All players initially agree on different "categories" that they want to play with (names, countries, cities, objects, ...).

  2. They define a duration to play each round, and the total amount of rounds that they want to play.

  3. They play each round with a specific letter, which chosen randomly by a player or by our website.

  4. During each round, all the players need to find one word per category. These words need to start with the letter chosen for the round.
  5. Each round stops at the end of the timer, or if one of the players found one word for each category before the end.

  6. At the end of each round, the players count their points as below, for each combination fo word and category:


    - When players have a unique word, they get 2 points.

    - When players have a repeated word (played by someone else in the same round), they get 1 point.

    - When players don't have any word, they don't get any point

  7. When all rounds are finished, all players sum all their point.
    The player with the highest amount of point wins!
