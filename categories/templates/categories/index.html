How to play categories?

1. Categories

All players initially agree on different "categories" that they want to play with (names, countries, cities, objects, ...).
2. Duration

They define a duration to play each round, and the total amount of rounds that they want to play.
3. Letter

They play each round with a specific letter, which can be chosen randomly by a player or by our website.
4. Playing

During each round, all the players need to find one word per category. These words need to start with the letter chosen for the round.
5. End of round

Each round stops at the end of the timer, or if one of the players found one word for each category before the end.
6. Counting points

At the end of each round, the players count their points as below, for each combination of word and category:
- When players have a unique word, they get 2 points
- When players have a repeated word (played by someone else in the same round), they get 1 point.
- When players don't have any word, they don't get any point
7. Final score

When all rounds are finished, all players sum all their point.
The player with the highest amount of point wins!
